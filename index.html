Once upon a time there was a group of theatre majors. They all hung out while they were in college. They would always hang out in an apartment right of campus, home of Maggie and Kenzy.

Then, after hanging out for so long, they decided they needed to formally organize. Thus, Team GBBO was born.

Though they have gone by many names and lived in many different areas over the years, their bond remains strong as ever.
